To launch the program and run tests, do the following: 

1) Load PyCharm IDE.
2) Import the files.
3) Run Driver.py.
4) Done.


Test: 

1) Enter the length of characters you would like the password to be.
2) Enter the site (and any other labels like usernames) with your password.
3) Click "Generate Password:" to generate the password. Leave the entry blank (or don't, idk).
4) Done! 

Retrieve Password: 

1) Copy the password that was printed into the file. It would be encrypted using the Caesar Cipher. 
2) Paste it into the encrypted password entry. 
3) Click "Retrieve Password" 
4) Take the decrypted password, and you're done! :)
